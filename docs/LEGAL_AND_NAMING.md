# Legal & Naming — Survival Trail: Frontier

## Hard rules (followed in all content)
- **Do NOT use** "Oregon Trail," its art, logos, font, official place/character names, or its specific copyrighted gags (e.g. the verbatim "You have died of dysentery" line).
- All world content is **original**: route = *The Ashford Reach*; start = *Fort Kestrel*; destination = *the Ashford valleys*. Characters (Mara Voss, Doc Renn, Tess Bowery, Old Hale, Junia Cole, Sam, Rhea, Boone, Lila) are original.
- Illness/death are modeled generically (trail fever, snakebite, bad water) — **no copied jokes or branded phrasing**.
- The genre itself (wagon/trail survival) is **not protectable**; only specific expression is. We keep our expression clean-room original.

## ASO/Trends note (direction, not demand)
Google Trends is **normalized search interest** — treat results as *relative direction*, not absolute volume. Intended comparison set (run in Trends UI; not callable from this environment):
`survival game` vs `strategy game` vs `simulation game` vs `frontier game` vs `trail game`.
Title leans on the higher-interest evergreen terms ("survival," "strategy," "simulation") while "Frontier" + "Trail" carry the theme. Re-run before each major store update and adjust the subtitle/keyword field, never the core title.
