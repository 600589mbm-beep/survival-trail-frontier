## Summary
Survival Trail: Frontier is a single-player, offline-first game. We collect the minimum needed to keep the game working and to improve it. We do not sell personal data.

## What the current build collects
- **Gameplay analytics (local):** anonymous event logs (run starts/ends, choices, session length) stored on your device (`analytics.jsonl`). No account, no name, no location.
- **No personal data, no precise location, no contacts, no microphone/camera.**
- Save data and purchase flags are stored locally on your device.

## What a released build will add (disclose only what ships)
- **Analytics SDK** (e.g. GameAnalytics/Firebase): anonymous usage + crash data.
- **Ads SDK** (rewarded only): may use a device advertising identifier; subject to OS-level consent (ATT on iOS, consent on Android).
- **In-app purchases:** processed by Apple/Google; we receive only a purchase confirmation, never card details.

## Children
Not directed at children under 13. Themes include survival peril and mild violence; rated accordingly.

## Your choices
- "Remove Ads" purchase disables ad-related collection.
- You can erase local save/analytics data in Settings → Erase saved run, or by uninstalling.
