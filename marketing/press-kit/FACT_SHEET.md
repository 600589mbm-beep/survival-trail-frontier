# Fact Sheet — Survival Trail: Frontier

- **Title:** Survival Trail: Frontier
- **Genre:** Trail-survival strategy / management RPG
- **Platforms:** iOS, Android
- **Price:** Free, with optional $4.99 Remove Ads + cosmetic skins (no pay-to-win)
- **Engine:** Godot 4.6
- **Modes:** Campaign (5 routes), Daily Challenge (shared seed)
- **Session length:** 8–15 minutes; offline; one-thumb portrait
- **Players:** Single-player
- **Languages at launch:** English

## Elevator pitch
Lead a five-person wagon party across a brutal, original frontier. Choose routes, ration food and water, treat sickness, repair the wagon, hunt, trade, and make hard calls — every day costs food, health, trust, or time. Permadeath, procedural events, and three endings make every crossing different.

## Key features
- 5 routes across 5 climates with a weather system
- 80+ branching events; moral choices with lasting consequences
- 5 starting party + 20 recruitable characters; morale & relationship bonds
- Named illnesses, injuries, hunting mini-game, trading posts, 3 wagon types
- Save anywhere, auto-save, fully offline
- Three endings; Daily Challenge runs

## Originality / legal
Original world ("The Reach"), original characters and locations. Not affiliated with or derived from any existing trail game; no protected IP. See repo `docs/LEGAL_AND_NAMING.md`.
