## "Survival Trail: Frontier" brings hard choices and a hungry wilderness to mobile

**[CITY, DATE]** — Indie developer **[Studio Name]** today launched **Survival Trail: Frontier**, a trail-survival strategy game for iOS and Android in which players lead a five-person wagon party across a brutal, original frontier where *every choice costs food, health, trust, or time.*

With 80+ procedural events, permadeath, and three endings, no two crossings play the same — and the game fits a single bus ride, offline, in one thumb.
