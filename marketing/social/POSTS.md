# Social & Community Post Drafts

> **Before posting to any subreddit, read its self-promotion rules** (most require a flair, a ratio of participation, or a weekly self-promo thread). Don't blast identical copy everywhere — tailor and space it out.

## Lead hook (use everywhere)
> Lead your wagon party across a brutal frontier where every choice costs food, health, trust, or time.

## TikTok / YouTube Shorts (caption + idea)
1. **"I let the whole party vote on every choice."** — screen-record a tense run, caption the choices, end on an ending screen. CTA: *Free. Offline. Link in bio.*
2. **"Ford the river or pay the ferryman?"** — freeze on the choice, poll the audience in caption, reveal the outcome.
3. **"Day 18: out of medicine, two sick, one storm coming."** — show the stat panel, narrate the dilemma in text overlays.
4. **"Every game I lose someone different."** — montage of 3 different "X did not make it" moments. Permadeath sells.

Format: vertical, first 1.5s shows the hook + a choice, hard cut, payoff by 6s. Repost the 15s trailer cut.
